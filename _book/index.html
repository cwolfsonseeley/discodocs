Welcome to the introduction and extended documentation for the discoveryengine! The discoveryengine, also known as “Disco Engine” or just “Disco,” is a tool enabling Prospect Development staff to do custom prospecting, written in the R programming language. Feel free to read this document in order, or to jump around or just bookmark it and refer to it as needed.
